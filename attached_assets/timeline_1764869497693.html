THE MEFA STORY

The Founding Vision

Started in early 1968, MEFA Needles was founded by the visionary Mr. Prem Nath Ji, whose foresight and dedication laid the foundation for a company that would revolutionize the hosiery industry in India.

Our trademark MEFA has served the hosiery industry for more than 50 years by providing a vast variety of Hand-Flat, Power-Flat, and circular knitting needles. What began as a small manufacturing unit has grown into the largest needle manufacturing group of Indian origin.

Mr. Prem Nath Ji's commitment to precision and customer satisfaction became the cornerstone of MEFA's philosophy, ensuring that every needle produced meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

MEFA's state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Ludhiana, Punjab, where precision meets innovation.

TIMELINE OF MEFA NEEDLES

1968

MEFA Needles Founded

Mr. Prem Nath Ji established MEFA Needles in Ludhiana, Punjab, with a vision to serve the hosiery industry with high-quality knitting needles. The company began manufacturing Hand-Flat knitting needles.

1975

Product Line Expansion

MEFA expanded its product range to include Power-Flat knitting needles, meeting the growing demands of the mechanized textile industry and establishing itself as a comprehensive needle supplier.

1982

Circular Knitting Needles Launch

Introduction of circular knitting needles marked a significant milestone, positioning MEFA as a complete solution provider for all types of knitting machinery in the hosiery sector.

1990

Quality Standards Achievement

MEFA achieved international quality certifications, reinforcing its commitment to precision manufacturing and establishing trust with customers across India and abroad.

1998

30 Years of Excellence

Celebrating three decades of service to the hosiery industry, MEFA had become synonymous with quality and reliability, serving thousands of customers nationwide.

2005

Manufacturing Technology Modernization

Investment in state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment and precision machinery enhanced production capacity and product quality, setting new industry benchmarks.

2010

Largest Needle Manufacturing Group

MEFA achieved the distinction of becoming the largest needle manufacturing group of Indian origin, a testament to decades of innovation and customer trust.

2018

Golden Jubilee - 50 Years

Celebrating 50 glorious years of serving the hosiery industry with unwavering commitment to quality, precision, and customer satisfaction. A legacy built on trust and excellence.

2020

Digital Transformation Initiative

Launch of online ordering platform and digital customer service, making MEFA products more accessible while maintaining the personal touch that customers have trusted for decades.

2025

Continuing the Legacy

Today, MEFA Needles continues to lead the industry with innovative products, sustainable manufacturing practices, and an unwavering commitment to the vision set by Mr. Prem Nath Ji over 50 years ago.
