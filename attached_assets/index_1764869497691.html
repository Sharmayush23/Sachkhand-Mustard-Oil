Five Decades of Excellence

Started in early 1968, our trademark MEFA has served the hosiery industry for more than 50 years by providing a vast variety of Hand-Flat, Power-Flat, and circular knitting needles.

Founded by the visionary Mr. Prem Nath Ji, MEFA Needles has grown to become the largest needle manufacturing group of Indian origin. His foresight and dedication laid the foundation for a company that prioritizes precision and customer satisfaction above all.
